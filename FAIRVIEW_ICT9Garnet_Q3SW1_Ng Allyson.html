Lycoris Recoil


Lycoris Recoil revolves around a group of characters that work for a secret organization of all-female agents. Their mission is to keep order and to make the environment as safe as possible. The anime mainly follows Takina Inoue and Chisato Nishikigi as they go on missions and live their daily lives.

Characters

Chisato Nishigiki


A elite Lycoris agent. Functions as an upperclassman to Takina Inoue. Works at the LycoReco cafe and is regaurded as one of the strongest agents of all time.

Takina Inoue


A quiet, cold, but talented Lycoris agent. Gets relocated from her previous place at the main headquarters to the LycoReco Cafe. Mostly takes on the role of a marksman.

Mika


The manager of the LycoReco Cafe. Had once worked as an agent, however he retired due to an injury sustained to his leg. A surrogate father to most of those at the cafe.
